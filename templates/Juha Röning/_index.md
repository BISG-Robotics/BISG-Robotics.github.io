Antti Tikanmäki	is the leader of BISG robotics group (2026-Now). He is also the founder and CTO of Probot Oy (2005-Now). He has published many technical papers at prominent journals and conferences. Moreover, he has practical experience in the robotics industry, such as the design of many industrial robots and the integration of robotic systems. His research topics are related to multiple sensors-based information representation and the design of modular robotic hardware and software.
